Privacy, cookies, and accessibility

A trusted AI site should be easy to inspect.

bast.ai is a static public website. It does not sell visitor data, run advertising pixels, or collect analytics unless a visitor chooses to allow analytics.

Analytics

Optional analytics use Google Analytics 4 to understand which pages, calls to action, outbound links, and inquiry actions are useful. Analytics are off until a visitor chooses "Allow analytics" in the cookie notice.

  • If analytics are allowed, the site may record page views and events such as CTA clicks, outbound clicks, and email inquiry clicks.
  • If "Necessary only" is selected, Google Analytics is not loaded by this site.
  • The Bast analytics measurement ID is __GA_MEASUREMENT_ID_DISPLAY__.

Cookies and local storage

The site stores one local preference, bast_cookie_consent, so it can remember whether a visitor chose analytics or necessary-only mode. If analytics are allowed, Google Analytics may set analytics cookies according to Google's controls and retention settings.

Video demos

Video demos are hosted by YouTube. The site does not load a YouTube player when the page first opens. A YouTube embed is loaded only after a visitor chooses "Load demo" or follows a YouTube link.

Contact and downloads

The site offers a contact form, gated PDF downloads, and email links. The contact form opens the visitor's own email app with the message pre-filled, so the visitor decides whether to send it. Gated downloads deliver the requested PDF to the visitor's device; if analytics are allowed, only the fact that a download or inquiry happened is recorded — never the email address entered. If a server-side submission endpoint is later configured to collect these details, this notice will be updated first.

Accessibility

The site is designed to be readable, keyboard navigable, and usable across screen sizes. It uses semantic HTML, visible focus states, descriptive links, and high-contrast body text. The working target is alignment with WCAG principles of perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust content.
